Code of Conduct

Let's Get Started

Communication is the foundation of every good working relationship. This is why I want to make sure that all of my potential clients and employers have a chance to get to know who I am, what I do, and what I'm about prior to beginning work together.

My Work

I take great pride in my work and would never put my name on a sub-par project. On the contrary, I will always make my best efforts to perform my services with a reasonable level of skill, judgement, and professionalism. I will be upfront about my capabilities and limitations.

My Partners: Clients and Employers

I value your privacy, have your best interests in mind, and your confidential information is safe with me.

Here's how:

  • I will not take on simultaneous projects which present a conflict of interest between competitors unless otherwise permitted.
  • I will never disclose confidential information (to the press or otherwise) regarding a client or employer, works in progress, or production methods unless required by law or given permission by the client.
  • I will not withdraw services without reasonable cause and adequate written notice.
  • I will carry out the terms of every contract into which I enter.

My Responsibility to Society and the Environment

No matter what I am doing, I strive to be as respectful as possible

Here's a bit about my personal morals:

  • I will not under any circumstances accept projects from clients or employers who advocate infringement on the human/property rights of others.
  • I will not accept any project which poses a deliberate disregard for the health, safety, or privacy of the community.
  • I will be careful to always portray society responsibly.
  • I will not devalue the profession of web development by working without compensation, with the exception of occasional projects for the community, charity, or family.

My Fees

There should never be any question as to what is being charged and why.

To avoid this scenario, I will fully disclose all service fees in a written contract with a clear run-down of services to be performed and the allocation of costs.

I will also inform clients when a requested service falls outside the scope of my basic service and will incur additional charges.

My Respect for Other 's Intellectual Property

As someone who wants others to respect the time and effort I put into my work, I am careful to do the same for others and will not infringe on the intellectual property of others.

As such:

  • I will not knowingly copy the design or content of another without their permission.
  • I will not pass off the work of others as my own.
  • I will not claim sole credit on any collaborative project.
