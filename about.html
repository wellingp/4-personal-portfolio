About Me

Hi! I'm Patti. I am a...

Torontonian, Web Developer, U of T Alumni, Visual Artist, Musician, etc.

When I Started

In the summer of 2020 I decided to update my professional skillset and, while trying to decide what I should do, I thought back fondly on my childhood days making Geocities fansites and how I was both good at it and enjoyed it. The idea that I could do something technical, yet creative, was very appealing to me.

And so, I enrolled in a Continuing Education Certificate Course with McMaster University and have been studying and working on projects ever since.

What I Do

I work primarily in front-end web development, with current, hands-on industry experience building and maintaining the Wordpress website for a medium-sized publishing company.

On a daily basis, I find myself:

  • Updating the website design and functionality.
  • Keeping website information up-to-date.
  • Designing marketing flyers, brochures, and promotional graphics in Photoshop and InDesign.
  • Designing mobile-first and responsive web designs that adhere to W3C and WCAG international web standards.
  • Regarding UX/UI best practices.
  • Programming JavaScript interactivity.

My style of design is retro and eccentric, muted yet colourful, and a delicate balance between light and dark.

How I Unwind

When I'm at home, you might find me working with my hands, making candles or sewing. I also like watching classic movies and sitcoms, studying (human) languages, and doing karaoke.

Otherwise, I play guitar as well and spend a lot of my time on music-related things. I am partial to rock, but listen to a bit of everything.

Outdoors, I like to go out and explore what the city has to offer. Going on park walks, going to concerts, and hitting up small movie venues.
