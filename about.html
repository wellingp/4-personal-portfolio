About Me

Hi! I'm Patti.

I am a Torontonian
Web Developer, U of T Alumni, Visual Artist, and Musician, among other things.

When I Started

In the summer of 2020 I decided to finally bite the bullet on web design after having it at the back of my mind for a long time. I remembered how I used to enjoy and excel at making Geocities fan sites when I was a kid and I thought I may as well give it a try. I enrolled in a Continuing Education certificate course with McMaster University and have been studying and working on projects ever since.

What I Do

My specialty lies in front-end development. From wireframing, to prototyping, to style guiding, to graphic designing; I feel most at home designing and building websites from the ground up, like a digital barn raising. Click here to see my stack, and here to see some of my projects.

My style of design is subtly retro and eccentric, clean, muted yet colourful, and a delicate balance between light and dark. I work in extremes and find ways to bring them to a mid-point that is modern and adaptable.

My dream stack includes the mastery of JavaScript and some back-end development using node.js.

How I Unwind

Outside of web development, I enjoy playing guitar, wishing I could play piano, and struggling to decide which (human) languages I want to learn.

When I go outside, I go to concerts, take walks around the city, and go to the movies.

Like what you hear? Feel free to drop me a line on any of my social media.
