Hi! I'm Patti. I am a Torontonian

About Me

Where It Started

In 2020 I decided to finally bite the bullet after considering web design for a long time. I needed a change and remembered how I used to enjoy and excel at making Geocities fan sites when I was a kid. I enrolled in a Continuing Education certificate course with McMaster University and have been studying and working on projects ever since.

What I Do

My specialty lies in front-end web development. From wireframing, to prototyping, to style guiding, to graphic designing - I feel most at home designing and building websites from the ground up, like a digital barn raising.

My style of design is based on a careful balance between retro and eccentric style elements, and modern web design trends. It is clean, subtle with colour, and effective. When working for a client, I approach in a similar way, instead striking a similar balance between their style and current practices. I believe that is my greatest strength which I bring to the party.

My dream stack includes the mastery of JavaScript and and some back-end development using node.js.
